Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer same-day appointments?
Yes, we accept same-day appointments based on availability. Call +91 9404336972 or use the booking form above. For urgent breathing issues, please visit our hospital immediately.
Do you provide online or video consultation?
No, we do not currently offer online or teleconsultation services. All consultations are conducted in-person at our hospital in Paratwada to ensure proper examination and accurate diagnosis.
What conditions are treated at the hospital?
We treat a wide range of conditions including respiratory problems (cough, asthma, COPD, bronchitis, pneumonia), tuberculosis (TB), diabetes, hypertension, fever, infections, and general health issues. We also offer preventive health checkups.
What diagnostic facilities are available?
Our hospital offers on-site blood tests, ECG (electrocardiogram), and spirometry (pulmonary function testing). Chest X-rays are available through our trusted diagnostic partner nearby.
What are the consulting hours?
Dr. Dubey is available for consultations from 11:00 AM to 7:00 PM, Monday to Saturday. The hospital is closed on Sundays.
Which areas do you serve?
We serve patients from Paratwada, Achalpur, Morshi, Chandur Bazar, Warud, Daryapur, Anjangaon Surji, and other areas in Amravati District. Our location at Jaistambh Chowk is centrally accessible.
Is parking available at the hospital?
Yes, convenient parking is available near the hospital at Jaistambh Chowk, Paratwada.
What should I bring for my first visit?
Please bring any previous medical reports, prescriptions, X-rays, or test results related to your condition. This helps Dr. Dubey understand your medical history better.
